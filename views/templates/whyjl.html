Why JOBLANA


Joblana is giving job opportunities in various sectors like human resources, office support, Calling (Inbound/Outbound), Digital Marketing, finance, accounting, creative services, Sales and marketing roles and IT Job roles(Software engineer, Systems analyst, Business analyst, Technical support, Web developer, Software tester).

JL Test is pen and paper based exam of 2 hours duration in which applicant have to solve 100 questions. These questions are divided in 4 Sections(Logical Reasoning, English Proficiency, Quantitative Aptitude, Domain Test for specific roles). JL Test consists specific roles exam which applicant choose according to their educational and professional background while scheduling the test date. There are certain sections which are compulsory for every applicant(Logical Reasoning, English Proficiency, Quantitative Aptitude).

Each section have 25 questions and all questions are of equal marks. Maximum marks in JL Test is 800. Each wrong answer takes a quarter of the marks assigned to that question from your total(There is marking of -1 marks for each wrong answer and +4 for each correct answer). The negative marking is small enough that if you are reasonably sure of an answer, you can take a chance and mark it on OMR marksheet. But be careful not to overdo it with guesses. Your accuracy could be the difference between selection and disqualification.

On the basis of applicants score in JL Test, an overall percentile is calculated for each candidate and applicants will be informed about their score through mail. Applicants can check their overall score and section-wise score on their JobLana account. Applicants have to pay one time fees of amount 900INR for appearing for JL Test. Last day to apply for any exam date is 48 hours before the scheduled date. Within 1 week of JL Test, Result will be declared on applicants profile. After that, it will take about 6-7 working days to get interview calls. Minimum qualification is 10+2 to appear for JL Test. Companies have vacancies for every profile whether you are 12th pass student, graduate , post-graduate or Phd scholar. Those who are willing to change their jobs can also appear for exam and get interview calls from startups and MNCs.
