Frequently Asked Questions about Joblana and JL Test

  • 1- What is JL Test?
    -JL Test(JobLana Test) is India's Largest Employability Assessment Test conducted by JobLana in most of metro cities in india and is recognised by over 500 companies.JL Test is a platform where job-seekers have access to hundreds of recruiters from across the country, based on a completely objective and standardised score, dramatically improving their chances of getting their dream job, with much less effort.
  • 2- What subjects does JL Test cover?
    -JL Test covers Logical Reasoning, English Proficiency, Quantitative Aptitude, Domain Test for specific roles.
  • 3- How much one have to pay for JL Test Exam ?
    -Applicants have to pay one time fees of amount 900INR for appearing for JL Test.
  • 4- How much time will it take to get interview calls after appearing for JL Test?
    -Within 1 week of JL Test, Result will be declared on applicants profile. After that , it will take about 6-7 working days to get interview calls.
  • 5- What is the pattern of JL Test ?
    -JL Test is of 2 hours duration in which applicant have to solve 100 questions. These questions are divided in 4 Sections(Logical Reasoning, English Proficiency, Quantitative Aptitude, Domain Test for specific roles). Each section have 25 questions and all questions are of equal marks. Maximum marks in JL Test is 800.
  • 6- Is JL Test is useful for those who are willing to change their jobs?
    -JobLana is a platform where Hundreds of companies are recruiting only experienced applicants through JL Test. So, Those who are willing to change their jobs can appear for exam and get interview calls from startups and MNCs.
  • 7- For which job roles, JL Test is helpful
    -JL Test is giving job opportunities in various sectors like human resources, office support, Calling (Inbound/Outbound), Digital Marketing, finance, accounting, creative services, Sales and marketing roles and IT Job roles(Software engineer, Systems analyst, Business analyst, Technical support, Web developer, Software tester).
  • 8- Which companies are recruiting through JobLana Platform?
    -Many startups and MNCs are recruiting on JL Scorecard like Paytm, Google, Microsoft, Apple, HP, Dell, Buyucoin, Desire4travels, Accenture, Makemytrip.
  • 9- How much time will it take to get placed after appearing for JL Test?
    -It actually depends on your performance during interviews but it is a surety that you will get interview calls within 15 days of appearing in JL Test.
  • 10- How often can I give a JL Test exam in 1 year?
    -If one is trying to improve its performance in exam or appearing for different job roles in JL Test then he can appear 4 times for JL Test exam in a year.
  • 11- What is the minimum qualification required to appear for JL Test?
    -Minimum qualification is 10+2 to appear for JL Test. Companies have vacancies for every profile whether you are 12th pass student, graduate , post-graduate or Phd scholar.
  • 12- What is the best time to write JL Test?
    -It depends on your preparation level for Exam. If you are ready today then this is the best time to appear for JL Test.
  • 13. Is there any negative marking in JL Test?
    - Yes, there negative marking of -1 marks for each wrong answer and +4 for each correct answer.
  • 14. What are the tricks to get more than no. of interview calls from companies ?
    If you score equally in all sections, then are more chances of getting calls from different companies .
