How It Works

  • 1- Firstly, Candidate have to register for JL Test on joblana.com and Fill up Job Profile to build a professional CV and then schedule a test date, test centre, and book a seat by paying 900 INR through online payment gateway.

  • 2- Candidate will receive admit card including exam center location and timing. From then, applicants will start getting new job updates continuously through mail.

  • 3- Candidate needs to bring the admit card and ID proof at the test centre on the test date.

  • 4- JL Test is pen and paper based exam of 2 hours duration and candidate have to submit OMR sheet to invigilator.

  • 5- Within 1 week of exam, Candidate will get result notification through mail and can check JL Score on its joblana account.

  • 6- After getting score, Applicant will be able to apply for various companies vacancies and automatic mail will be forwarded to recruiters within seconds after applying for positions.

  • 7- Within 6-7 working days, Applicant will start getting interview calls from startups and MNCs.
