JL Test

What is JL Test?

-JL Test(JobLana Test) is India's Largest Employability Assessment Test conducted by JobLana in most of metro cities in india and is recognised by over 500 companies.JL Test is a platform where job-seekers have access to hundreds of recruiters from across the country, based on a completely objective and standardized score, dramatically improving their chances of getting their dream job, with much less effort.

JL Test is pen and paper based exam of 2 hours duration in which applicant have to solve 100 questions. These questions are divided in 4 Sections(Logical Reasoning, English Proficiency, Quantitative Aptitude, Domain Test for specific roles). Each section have 25 questions and all questions are of equal marks. Maximum marks in JL Test is 800.

Applicants have to pay one time fees of amount 900INR for appearing for JL Test. Within 1 week of JL Test, Result will be declared on applicants profile. After that, it will take about 6-7 working days to get interview calls. Minimum qualification is 10+2 to appear for JL Test. Companies have vacancies for every profile whether you are 12th pass student, graduate , post-graduate or Phd scholar. Those who are willing to change their jobs can appear for exam and get interview calls from startups and MNCs.
